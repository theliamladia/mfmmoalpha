Mint Your Own Altcoin

One at a time, name it whatever you want
10 FC mint

Any player can mint an altcoin for 10 FC, creating a 1,000-coin total supply under a custom name of your choosing. From there, the market decides what it's worth.

Coin price as supply sells out0.10 FC → 0.15 FC
🟢 Early buy-in🟡 Mid-run🔴 Full sellout

Anyone can see how many coins are left in a run. Nobody — not even the minter — can see who else holds coins or what they paid. Whoever holds the largest stake at any moment controls the coin's fate:

💥 Rug It

Dump your holding at any time (or the instant the coin fully sells out) and the price craters to ~5–10% of its peak. Everyone still holding eats the loss. You walk away with the whole pool.

🤝 Full Buyout

Only available right at 1,000/1,000 sold — liquidate the entire coin back to Floydcoin and pay every holder pro-rata at the final price. The honest ending. Nobody gets left holding the bag.

📊 Worked Example — "KroogCoin"

Minted for 10 FC. Owner immediately buys 501 of the 1,000 coins at mint. The other 499 sell out to the rest of the server.

🤝 Plays It Fair
+2.5 FC
~4% return on his stake — sells his own position at the normal declining-price curve
💥 Rugs Everyone
+58.6 FC
~88% return — ~23x bigger payday than playing fair, at the direct expense of the other 499-coin holders
⚠️ The math will (almost) always tempt you to rug. That's the point. Buy into someone else's coin knowing exactly what you're signing up for.
