    📖 Wiki

    A quick rundown of every page and how to use it.

    🛣️ Da Skreetz

    Instant-payout hustles on a 10 second cooldown. Work is safe and pays $2–$10. Slut pays $5–$60 with a 30% chance of getting robbed for the same amount, and nudges your Alliance toward Bad. Crime pays $100–$1,000 with a 30% chance of landing in Jail; getting caught sends your Alliance straight to Bad (or worse) instantly, and your criminal record grows, adding a year to every future sentence (from any crime) until you pay it down with Community Service.

    🏬 Milos Market

    Plan-it Fitness burns weight for Speed/Defense gains — pick a Steroid cycle (Mild, Standard, or Heavy) for bigger gains at the cost of worse Roid Jail odds and longer sentences. Pete's Pies and Luke's Maxxerstore trade weight and cash for Looks; pricier items always cost more per point, never less, so there's no "cheap trap" option. Cosmetixxx sells titles and gacha crates (OPEN BETA $5,000, GOOD® Season 1 $10,000). Max every stat to 100 to earn the PEAK CIVILIAN title. Looks speeds up job skill training, Height and Speed help you in a fight, Speed/Defense help you dodge a bust on the job, and high HP means you keep more cash when you lose a fight.

    🎰 Midas Casino

    Convert Floydbucks to Chips at the Cashier, then play Blackjack or Slots. Chips only exist in the Casino — cash back out at the Cashier when you're done.

    🌆 New Milos City

    Hustles splits into Good Hustles (small steady legal cash, Neutral alliance or better) and Bad Hustles (drug running, dealing, and robbery — Bad alliance required, high risk of jail). Both job tracks use a promotion ladder: as your average skill climbs, each rank has its own pay band (the floor rises faster than the ceiling, so higher ranks pay more and more consistently) plus a shorter cooldown. Reaching Supervisor/Lieutenant unlocks a job-specific perk — Milos11 knocks 20% off Pete's Pies, Pete'sza Delivery grants a permanent +2 Speed, Krogue Wrestler Gear unlocks the exclusive Wrestling Gear Store, Getaway Driver cuts your bust chance, and The Fence knocks 15% off the Gun Club. Combat is turn-based: Punch (reliable), Heavy Strike (bigger damage, can whiff), Guard (blocks most damage, can riposte), and Weapon Attack (biggest damage if you're carrying a weapon) — Height and equipped weapons/gear boost your Attack, Speed lets you dodge outright, HP protects your cash if you lose, and a win has a chance to grant a small permanent Attack or HP boost. Crime is a set of fixed-payout crimes (Shoplifting up through Grand Theft Auto) where Attack and Speed lower your odds of getting caught; getting caught here (or anywhere) snaps your Alliance straight to Bad and escalates every future sentence until you spend cash on Community Service. City Hall handles renames, marriage, and the Gun Safety Course. NMC Gun Club sells guns, ammo, melee weapons (no license needed), a shooting range, and carry licenses — you need the Gun Safety License from City Hall before you can buy a gun. Bank keeps your cash safe from robbery, lets you upgrade to hold more, and offers a credit card billed every 24 real-life hours. Morals Center lets you pick a standing stance — Accept Ricardo, Renounce Ricardo, or Invoke Neutrality — that quietly nudges your Alliance every 10 seconds, even on other pages. Milos Trading Network is the player marketplace: list anything from your Inventory for sale, and once it sells the Floydbucks land in your account and the sale logs to your MTN history. New Milos Penitentiary shows who's currently locked up (and for what), plus a recent-arrests history — post bail or send commissary money for their books.

    🎒 Character

    Inventory is split into Licenses, Items, and Cosmetics. Equipment has an 8-slot board — guns and melee weapons in the holster/open-carry/melee slots, and exclusive Wrestling Gear (from the Krogue Wrestler Gear job) in the helmet/chest/pants/feet slots for extra combat stats. Carrying a gun without the right license or permit risks arrest — check the law banner at the top of New Milos City. Melee weapons (brass knuckles, knives) need no license and carry no arrest risk. Skills shows XP bars for your current jobs, weapon skills, and drug-dealer reputation. Alignment shows exactly where you sit on the Good–Bad spectrum plus your overall level/stat progress toward PEAK CIVILIAN.

    ⛓️ Jail

    Only reachable if you're arrested — all other pages grey out. Serve Time removes 1 year every 10 seconds. Hire Lawyer pays cash for immediate release. Doing time doesn't have to be dead time: Yard Workout is a free, repeatable Attack/Defense gain, Yard Fight is an instant-resolve scrap against another inmate for a stat boost (or a cash shakedown if you lose), and Contraband lets you smuggle in melee weapons and drugs at a markup so they're waiting in your Inventory when you're out. Repeat offenses raise your criminal record and lengthen future sentences — use Community Service (New Milos City → Crime) once you're free to pay it down. New Milos City → New Milos Penitentiary is the public side of all this: see who's currently locked up (and for what), post their bail, or send them money for their books.
